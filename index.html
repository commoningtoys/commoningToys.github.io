Dr. Viktor Bedö

Since summer 2010 Viktor Bedö is part of the teaching team of the School of Design Thinking at the Hasso-Plattner-Institute. Viktor is member of Invisible Playground, a Berlin based Street- and Urban-Games collective creating playful experiences in various cities in Germany and world wide. Viktor earned a PhD degree with his thesis "Interactive Urban Maps as Instruments of Thinking" in a double doctoral scheme including the Doctor School for Philosophy, University of Pécs and the Institute for Philosophy at the Humboldt University of Berlin.His is interested in community driven urban mapping, embodied urban knowledge, visual thinking, urban games, design thinking and contemporary dance.
